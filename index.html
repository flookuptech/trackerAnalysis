PRODUCTS AND SERVICES


1. Tally ERP 9


Tally ERP 9 comes in 2 variants. The silver version is a single user license and gold version is multi-user license(in LAN environment). Tally.ERP 9, a software that grows with you. You are assured that your needs, as you grow are easily handled. With Tally, you can manage multiple companies and incrementally add features such as multiple go-downs, multi-currency, order process, cost centres etc. This helps you in getting rid of complexities, and in turn, focus on business growth.

2. Tally Server 9


Tally Server 9 offers powerful data server capabilities over the Gold license. This server-based Data Architecture unleashes greater concurrency, secured data access, and advanced monitoring capabilities. Tally.Server 9 is truly an Enterprise Class product that helps fast-growing medium and large businesses improve their business efficiencies.

3. Tally Software Services


Tally Software Services (TSS) is a software subscription for a collection of services which add great value to your Tally.ERP 9 by giving you the latest developments in technology and statutory laws. Continuous product upgrades and Updates, connectivity driven functionalities such as online data exchange between your branches, remote access, seamless banking and payment services and much more, enhance your business performance by leaps and bounds.

4. Tally on Cloud


Maintaining financial records and accounting data are never easy especially when it comes to Tally ERP 9. Tally on cloud hosting services with which you can now access your Tally from any device, anytime.

5. Tally Customization


The default Tally.ERP9 is packed with powerful functionalities & feature which would address 90% of the businesses. The balance unique requirements can be incorporated by customizing Tally. In a way Tally provides best of both the world i.e. Packages software & customized software.

6. Annual Maintenance Contract


A business continuity support system for your organisation from a team of experts who knows Tally Best. We ensure our support even beyond the normal business hours & holidays while you continue your work peacefully. We provide Unconditional Tally support through telephone, email support, chat support as well as unlimited Onsite Visits in our Onsite Support plan.
